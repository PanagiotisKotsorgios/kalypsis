IMPORTANT CAVEAT
----------------
The Greek insurance market consolidated heavily in recent years:
  • ATE Insurance       → absorbed by ERGO Hellas
  • AXA Greece          → absorbed by Generali Hellas
  • Eurolife ERB        → renamed to Eurolife FFH
  • ING Greece          → renamed to NN Hellas
  • Imperio (life)      → various reshuffles
Treat this list as a STARTING CHECKLIST, not a canonical registry.

Before mail-merging:
  1. Pull the current member list from EAEE (Ένωση Ασφαλιστικών Εταιριών
     Ελλάδος) at https://www.eaee.gr — it's the authoritative directory.
  2. For each name below, verify the company still trades under that name
     in Greece and find the right partnerships address (see "addressing"
     section at the bottom).
  3. Dedupe — many entries below are the same legal entity rebranded.

C. LLOYD'S / SYNDICATES (broker-distributed, not single email targets)
==============================================================================

Lloyd's coverholders operating in Greece work through the Lloyd's General
Representative office in Athens for regulatory matters, then through
individual syndicates via brokers. There isn't a single email target —
skip until you have an established broker relationship channeling the
introductions.

==============================================================================
ADDRESSING — which inbox to use
==============================================================================

For each carrier, three address types typically exist:

  info@<carrier>          → general inbox, gets dropped to assistants.
                            AVOID for partnership initiation.

  broker(s)@<carrier>     → broker-relations desk. USUALLY THE RIGHT ONE.
  synergates@<carrier>      Try this first.
  agents@<carrier>

  partnerships@<carrier>  → for the API / integration angle.
  it@<carrier>              Try this second.

Recommended pattern:
  • Send TO  : partnerships@ or broker(s)@
  • CC       : the alternate one of the above
  • Skip     : info@
